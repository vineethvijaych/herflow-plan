Executive Summary core

What is HerFlows?

A predictive period care subscription that delivers personalized kits before your cycle starts. The period tracker is NOT the product — the subscription system is. Tracking only powers predictions.

Why different from Flo & Clue?
We deliver, they don’t. We predict, they track.
Recurring revenue engine
Subscription = predictable LTV.
