    Executive Summary

    What is HerFlows?

    HerFlows is a subscription-based personalized period care service that delivers customized kits to women every month based on their predicted cycle. Users track their cycle, our prediction engine forecasts their next period, and a tailored kit of pads, liners, heating patches, chocolates, and tea arrives before they need it.

    Why are we building it?
    Women make last-minute trips to the store every month. There is no automatic, personalized solution that delivers period care before it's needed. We're building convenience, personalization, and reliability into a recurring subscription model.
    Why is this different from Flo & Clue?
    Flo and Clue are tracker apps. They help women log cycles but stop at prediction. HerFlows connects prediction to physical delivery. We don't just tell you when—we make sure you have what you need before it arrives.
    💡

    The period tracker is NOT the product. The subscription system is the product. Tracking only powers predictions.

    Business Strategy

    Phase 1 — Launch with Trusted Brands
    We DO NOT manufacture initially. We source from authorized distributors of Stayfree, Whisper, Sofy, Nua, and Carefree. Products arrive sealed in original packaging. We pack inside HerFlows branded boxes. This eliminates quality risk and regulatory hurdles.
    Phase 2 — Build Trust & Data
    As we accumulate subscribers, we learn preferences, buying patterns, and price sensitivity. This data informs our private label strategy. We build brand credibility through consistent quality and premium unboxing experience.
    🏗️

    Strategy: Start with zero manufacturing risk. Build trust. Learn from data. Then introduce private label in Phase 3.

    Strategic Roadmap

    Phase 1 — Q4 2025
    Market Validation & Sourcing
    Distributor tie-ups, MVP dashboard, initial 10–15 SKU launch, 100 beta subscribers

    🏭 Manufacturer
    Procter & Gamble (Whisper), Johnson & Johnson (Stayfree), Unicharm (Sofy), Nua — all produce at scale with ISO certifications. We buy from authorized distributors, never directly from manufacturers initially.
    📦 Authorized Distributor
    Distributors like Vora Health, MomsCo, and regional FMCG distributors hold wholesale inventory. We negotiate monthly offtake at 15–25% below MRP. Minimum order: 50 units per SKU.
    🏢 HerFlows Warehouse
    Rented 500 sq ft warehouse in suburban industrial area. Shelving, barcode scanners, packing tables. Monthly rent: ₹25,000–35,000. Temperature-controlled storage for chocolates.
    🧩 Kit Assembly
    Each kit is assembled per subscriber preference. Flow type determines pad quantity. Brand choice respected. Add-ons (chocolate, tea, heating patch) included per plan.
    ✅ Quality Check
    Every kit visually inspected before sealing. Expiry date checked. Packaging integrity verified. Damaged items replaced from inventory buffer stock.
    🚚 Courier
    Partner with Shiprocket/Delhivery for pan-India shipping. Average delivery: 3–5 days. Tracking shared via WhatsApp. Aim to dispatch 5 days before predicted period.

    SE Asia, Middle East
    Phase 1 — Now
    Launch with Stayfree, Whisper, Sofy, Nua. Zero manufacturing. Pack in HerFlows branded boxes. Build subscriber base. Validate product-market fit with 3 plan tiers.
    Phase 3 — Private Label
    Once we hit 2,000+ subscribers, we introduce HerFlows-branded pads manufactured by contract partners. Margin improves from ~40% to ~55–60%. Brand equity grows.
    Phase 4 — Marketplace
    Open platform for wellness brands: Ayurvedic supplements, organic menstrual cups, period underwear, fertility trackers. Commission-based revenue.
    Phase 5 — International
    Target markets: UAE (high disposable income, expat women), Singapore (e-commerce penetration), Indonesia (large young female population). Localize kits.
